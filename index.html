Casualties

How typhoons can affect people

  • 17+ people killed by floods

    According to the news sources, over 17 people were killed by floods and landslides after Typhoon Parma struck the Philippines.

  • 930 homes affected

    Ilocos Norte reported 924 homes were affected by the flood, while Benguet reported 6 homes damaged by landslides

  • 9.3+ million people affected

    Over 9.3 million people were affected by Typhoon Pepeng along with Typhoon Ondoy. It claimed 956 lives, 700 were injured, and left 84 people missing.



Solutions

How does one conquer these typhoons?

  • Be prepared

    Have a preparedness plan. This is to ensure that you know what steps you should take in case of emergency. Charge your devices, and have a preparedness kit.

  • Always stay updated

    Preparedness won't come in handy if you don't know what's going on around you. Nowadays, you can stay updated by listening to radio, watching TV, and reading articles from trusted news sources online.

  • Seek a safe place to stay

    During a typhoon, seek a safe place to evacuate. Preferrably, underground. Listen for updates in the radio, and wait for updates on when you can safely go out.

About TyphoonWatch

TyphoonWatch is a project from the Tranch Research Institute with a goal of mitigating the effects of typhoons.

In this site, we cover how we could mitigate the effects of typhoons, preparing for the typhoon, and what to do during a typhoon.
